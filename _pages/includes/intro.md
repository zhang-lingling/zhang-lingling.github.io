Lingling Zhang（张玲玲）is an associate professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Xi'an Jiaotong University, and a member of Shaanxi Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Knowledge Engineering (陕西省大数据知识工程重点试验室，学术带头人郑庆华院士). She received joint Ph.D. training at the Carnegie Mellon University （卡耐基梅隆大学） from September 2018 to September 2019 and earned his Ph.D. from Xi’an Jiaotong University in July 2020. 
Her research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence technologies such as knowledge graphs, large language models and computer vision in smart education.
